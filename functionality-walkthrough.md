# User Guide - Candlestick Alert System

## Getting Started
1. **Login**: Use the Replit Auth button on the landing page to securely log in.
2. **Dashboard**: View a high-level summary of total stocks and active signals.
3. **Running Scans**: Click "Run Manual Scan" on the dashboard to update stock signals immediately.

## Understanding Signals
- **Green (Bullish)**: Indicates the stock closed higher than it opened at the start of the month.
- **Red (Bearish)**: Indicates the stock closed lower than it opened.
- **Signal Logic**: The app specifically looks for "engulfing" patterns where a month's move overcomes the starting point of the previous opposite month.

## Alerts & Notifications
- Navigate to the **Alerts** tab to see a history of generated signals.
- Use the **Settings** page to enable email notifications via the configured email service.
